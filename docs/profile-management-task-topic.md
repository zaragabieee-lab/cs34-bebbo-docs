# Profile Creation 

Upon installing Bebbo on a device, the app will prompt users to create a profile immediately. For the parent profile, the following must be provided: 

- Role in the child’s life.
- Gender of the user.
- Name of the user's nickname. 

For the child profile, the following must be provided: 

- The child's name or nickname.
- Birth date.
- Gender. 

Therefore, managing the profile will require action after the account creation. 

## Profile Management For the Child 

1. Click the **pencil icon** on the child’s introductory tab shown on the upper part of the Home page. It may also indicate that the child's profile is *“incomplete.”*

**Note: The following instruction is for your discretion.**

2. Click the camera icon to upload the child’s photo.
3. Enter the child’s updated name or nickname. 
4. Update the child’s birth date or due date. 
5. Update the child’s gender. 
6. Save details.

## Profile Management for the User 

1. Click the child’s icon in the upper right corner of the app. 
2. Go to **“Manage Profile "** and click the pencil icon beside the second tab where “Parents’ detail” is indicated. 
3. Click the first tab indicating your relation to the child (i.e., mother, father, other caregiver, or service provider.)
4. Choose the gender, and enter your name. 
5. Save details. 

# Profile Management More Update 

### A. Add Another Child 

1. Click the child’s icon in the upper right corner of the app. 
2. Go to **“Manage Profile "** and click **“ + Add Another Child “ ** 
3. Click the camera icon to upload the child’s photo. 
4. Enter the child’s given name. 
5. Enter the child’s birth date. 
6. Enter the child’s gender.
7. Save details.

### B. Add Expected Child 

1. Click the child’s icon in the upper right corner of the app. 
2. Go to **“Manage Profile "** and click **“ + Add Expected Child “ ** 
3. Enter Due Date 
4. Enter Preferred Name.
5. Save details.
