# Notifications 


You, parents and caregivers can get reminded about important updates on their child's health, development, and upcoming appointments.


## Purpose

Notifications help users stay updated on:

- Vaccination schedules.
- Health check-ups.
- Development milestones.
- App updates.

## Notification Types

### Child's Growth

Alerts users regarding:

- Growth tracking updates.
- Height measurement reminders.
- Weight measurement reminders.
- Growth assessment recommendations.

### Child Development 

Alerts users regarding:

- New milestones.
- Recommended activities.
- Developmental achievements.
  
### Vaccinations

Alerts users regarding:

- Upcoming vaccines.
- Overdue vaccines.
- Recently completed vaccinations.

### Health Check-ups

Alerts users regarding:

- Upcoming appointments.
- Follow-up consultations.
- Health assessment schedules.

## Viewing Notifications

1. Open Bebbo.
2. Tap the **Notifications** icon.
3. Review the notification list.

## Managing Notifications

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Select **Notifications**.
3. Enable or disable notification preferences.
4. Save changes.

<img width="1200" height="630" alt="4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c4bcc360-fa26-4e88-89ff-06ee29bd2637" />

### Navigating Notifications:
1. Open the Notification 
2. Choose amongst the rectangular options which to be viewed first 
3. Choose as much as desired to view. 
4. Tap again on the tool to deselect it from the view
<img width="1200" height="630" alt="6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2172f65e-d570-405c-b30c-400ec3ed35bf" />


#### Note: The tools opened will be indicated according to the color assigned:
- Violet - Child's Growth
- Teal Green - Vaccinations
- Yellow - Child Development 
- Pink - Health Check-up

## Best Practices

- Keep notifications enabled.
- Review reminders regularly.
- Update child information to receive accurate recommendations.
