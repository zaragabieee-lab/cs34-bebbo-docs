# Landing Page

## Understanding the landing page

The landing page serves as the central dashboard of the Bebbo Parenting App.

It brings together important information about your child's health, growth, and development in a single location.

## Reviewing child information

The child profile summary provides a quick overview of your child's information.

The summary may display:

- Child name.
- Age.
- Development stage.

Reviewing this information helps you stay aware of your child's current developmental journey.

## Accessing core modules

The landing page provides access to:

- **Child Development**
- **Vaccinations**
- **Health Checkups**
- **Child Growth**
- **Notifications**

Click any module to explore more detailed information.

## Reviewing important reminders

The landing page highlights reminders that may require your attention.

Reminders may include:

- Upcoming vaccines.
- Upcoming health checkups.
- Development milestone updates.

Review reminders regularly to stay informed about important activities.

## Exploring personalized recommendations

Bebbo provides recommendations based on your child's age and developmental stage.

Recommendations may include:

- Parenting guidance.
- Learning activities.
- Health information.
- Development resources.

These recommendations help support your child's growth and development.

## Supporting everyday parenting

The landing page helps you:

- Access important information quickly.
- Monitor developmental progress.
- Review health-related updates.
- Navigate between modules efficiently.

The dashboard keeps key information available whenever you need it.
