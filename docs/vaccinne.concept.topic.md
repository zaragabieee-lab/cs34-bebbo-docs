
# Vaccinations
The **Vaccinations** tool is designed to help you monitor the vaccinations administered on your child or children. It involves the due dates for dosing their child or children and any missed appointments. Furthermore, each supplement has information about its purpose and the diseases it prevents. 

### Sections
1. Vaccination Summary
2. Vaccination Schedule

## Navigating Vaccination Schedule
### Setting Reminder for Vaccination Schedule

<img width="1200" height="630" alt="3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f138f08d-82ca-4204-8cc7-1bae44fc528b" />

1. Click **Upcoming**

 <img width="1200" height="630" alt="4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/24d9b9e3-f053-477d-835f-6911c10fee7f" />

2. Scroll to your child's birth month, and then click it.
<img width="1200" height="630" alt="5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ac2345cf-1211-4335-ad0a-9915fb4b0624" />

3. Click **Set Vaccination reminder**

  <img width="1200" height="630" alt="6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dde41aab-8f52-421b-8919-356fc062f741" />

4. Enter the date and time for the vaccination schedule
