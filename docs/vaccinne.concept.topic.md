
# Vaccinations
The **Vaccinations** tool is designed to help you monitor the vaccinations administered on your child or children. It involves the due dates for dosing their child or children and any missed appointments. Furthermore, each supplement has information about its purpose and the diseases it prevents. 

### Sections
1. Vaccination Summary
2. Milestone

## Vaccination Summary
It lists the administered vaccine, to be administered vaccines, and missed vaccines
## Vaccination Schedule
It states the month of the parents' child or children with the expected vaccine, the next vaccine schedule, and which vaccine to be administered

## Navigating Vaccination Schedule
### Setting Reminder for Vaccination Schedule
1. Click on **upcoming**
2. Scroll to your child's birth month, and then click it.
3. Click **Set Vaccination reminder**
4. Enter the date and time for the vaccination schedule
5. Enter the date and time when you want to be reminded of the vaccination schedule
6. Click **save**
### Logging Previous Vaccination Schedule
1. Click on **previous**
2. Scroll to your child's birth month, and then click it.
3. Click on the **Enter vaccines received**
4. Enter the **health check-up date**
5. Tick the vaccines administered to your child on the checklist
6. Click **save**

### Exploring Other Features of Vaccines
- Choose 'Yes' on **Was the child measured?** to log your child's current weight and height
- Enter the doctor's remarks, if there are suggestions to improve your child's health
- Click **Read more about this vaccine** to see deeper insights about the vaccines
- Click on **share**  on the left side of the screen to **share** on social media platforms about the vaccine, if desired.
- Click the **save**  on the right side of the screen to store the information in the **Favorites**, if desired
- Click the **related advice** to read more about the vaccines and other related topics about it, if desired
- Tap on the **left arrow** to go back to the Vaccines page once done reading about the desired information
