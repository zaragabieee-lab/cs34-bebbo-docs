# Child’s Development

The “Child’s Development” tool is the most interactive, informative, and monitoring feature of the Bebbo parenting app. It includes a child tracker from the first month through age 7, a YouTube video, a milestone tracker, and tips from Doctors.

## Components 

1. Child’s Age
2. YouTube Video. 
3. Milestone Percentage Tracker. 
4. Milestone Checklist Tracker. 
5. Tips from Doctors. 

## Child’s Age 
It has a dedicated **title** for each month (i.e., 1st month: getting to know each other). Its tracking age is also in **‘months’** to have a continuous tracker (i.e., 13-18 months = 1 year old; 19-24 months = 2 years old; 25-36 months = 3 years old, and so on until age 7).

## YouTube Videos 
It provides a corresponding two-minute video from Bebbo’s YouTube app to visualize the expected behavior of your child or children, in the chosen month. 

## Milestone Percentage Tracker. 
It shows a visual milestone achievement as a percentage and completes the circle when all expected behavior has been checked off, with “Complete” and congratulatory greetings; if not, it shows a “Pending” with an exclamation mark beside it. 

## Milestone Checklist Tracker. 
It provides expected behaviors your child should exhibit when it’s the correct month, a corresponding milestone YouTube video, and related games to guide the child’s expected actions. 

## Tips from Doctors 
It presents various information and tips from doctors in a single line, in order. 

# Navigating Child Development Tool

It can be found in two locations: 

On the lower bar, beside “Advice.” 
Click on the “Tools” button via the sidebar. 

Regardless, it will take you to the Child’s Development interface. 

1. Choose the correct month your baby is in. 
2. Click the YouTube video for your guidance. 
3. Check off the expected behaviors. 
4. Explore the milestone tracker section and its related games. 
5. Review the doctor’s tips and track your child’s development. 
6. Click the next month properly.
