# Installation of the Bebbo App
Before you install, make sure you have:
- A smartphone or tablet with internet access.
- A valid email address or supported sign-in method.
- Basic information about your child, such as date of birth and gender.

## In an Android mobile:
1. Go to the **‘Play Store’** **('Google Play')**.
2. Type in for the **‘Bebbo App’** in the **Search**  below the page.
3. Click on the **Install**.
4. Wait for the download to finish.
5. Click the **Open** once the download finishes.

### Note: You can download the app on another mobile from your personal mobile through:
1. Go to the **‘Play Store’** **('Google Play')**
2. Register the Gmail Account of the owner of another mobile in the **‘Play Store’** **('Google Play)**
3. Type in for the **‘Bebbo App’** in the **Search** below the page
4. Click the **drop-down** arrow, and then select the desired mobile to download the app.
5. Wait for the download to finish.
6. Click the **Open** once the download finishes

## In an Apple mobile:
1. Go to the **App Store**
2. Type in for the **Bebbo App** in the **Search** below the page
3. Click on the **Install**
4. Wait for the download to finish
5. Click the **Open** once the download finishes
