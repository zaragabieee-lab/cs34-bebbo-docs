#Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

#Overview:

Bebbo answers the frequently asked questions (FAQs) that brand new parents and users of the app. The following series of questions and answers guide you to maximize Bebbo's features by section. 

#Section 1: **Child Development**

###1. How do I update my profile? 

To update your profile, click the baby icon on the top left corner beside the notification bell, and then click on "Manage Profile."

###2. How can I reach 100% on the "Milestone" sections? 

To reach 100% on the **Milestone** section, check the boxes that aligns with yout baby's behaviour at the lower part of the **Child's Development** section.
