# Know More about the Tools in Bebbo Bebbo App:


Bebbo App has a specific tracker for a newborn’s milestones: Child Development, Vaccines, Health Check-up, and Child’s Growth 

Child Development has the expected motor activities of your child. On the page, you can see a checklist of the physical activities your child performed during the month. On the Vaccines page, you can view the supplements to help prevent potential harm to your child. It includes the schedules for administering and information about the vaccines. Health Check-up allows you to set reminders for doctor’s appointments and keeps a log of your doctor’s visits.  Lastly, Child Growth is dedicated to monitoring your child's physical growth. 

These tools are to help you, parents and caregivers, be informed on the needs of your child or children to help them grow, healthier and happier. Bebbo  App is your friend that helps you provide the best care for your beloved!
