# Managing Users’ Data in Bebbo 

You need to keep your data safe, secure, and entirely on your own. Hence, follow the instructions below on backing up and saving data. 

## Back Up and Upload Data
<img width="1200" height="630" alt="3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f2dc789e-a26d-420e-820c-3d76080e3b35" />

1. Click  the sidebar of the app. 
2. Go to Settings, and then swipe down where “Transfer Data” is visible. 
3. Click **Back up your data** and choose where to save your data:

**File Manager or Google Drive.**

### Back Up Data For File Manager: 

1. Choose where the file will be saved in your device (i.e, Downloads). 
2. Create a folder specifically for Bebbo files.
3. Rename your “mybackup” file to your chosen title, and then click save. 
4. Upload the saved data from File Manager. 

### Back Up Data For Google Drive: 

1. Sign in with your chosen Google Account. 
2. Allow Bebbo to access your information
3. Click Continue, and check your Google Drive account.

  Bebbo will automatically create a folder in your drive named **ParentBuddy.** 

4. Upload the saved data from Google Drive account.
